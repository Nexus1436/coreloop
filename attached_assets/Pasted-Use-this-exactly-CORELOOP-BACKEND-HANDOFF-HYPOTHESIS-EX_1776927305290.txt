## PROBLEM A — HYPOTHESIS EXTRACTION MISS

### Observed failure

A legitimate assistant explanation was produced, but no hypothesis was stored.

Example pattern:

* assistant gives a real mechanical explanation
* explanation is specific enough to count
* database still shows no hypothesis

### Important clarification

The hypothesis should be captured from the **assistant’s mechanism sentence**, not from the later user thank-you message.

### What this means

The extraction gate is too narrow or too dependent on a small set of trigger phrases.

### 1. Preserve quality

Do **NOT** turn every assistant sentence into a hypothesis.

Do **NOT** accept:

* generic coaching filler
* vague success language

* closure language
* “the key is…”
* “this should help…”
* “good sign…”
* motivational filler
* simple acknowledgments

The stored hypothesis must still be:

* singular
* causal or explanatory
* mechanical

### 2. Expand valid mechanism phrasing support

Right now the system appears too dependent on narrow trigger phrases like:

* because
* due to
* driven by
* caused by
* the issue is
* the problem is
* is breaking
* is collapsing

Expand support so valid assistant mechanism sentences still qualify when they are clearly explanatory even if they use other legitimate Coreloop phrasing.

* reduced X during Y, leading to Z
* loss of X during Y, causing Z

* “which was affecting…”
* “leading to…”
* “resulting in…”
* “contributing to…”
* “reducing… and causing…”

The core rule:
If the sentence clearly explains **what is happening physically and why the complaint is occurring**, it should have a path to qualify.
