PULSE / BREATH TIMING INSTRUCTIONS

Overall rhythm
	•	Full breath cycle: 8.5 seconds total
	•	Inhale (contracting): 4.5 seconds
	•	Pause at smallest point: 0.5 seconds
	•	Exhale (expanding): 3.5 seconds

Inhale behavior (orb contracting)
	•	Orb contracts slowly and continuously
	•	Density increases as it gets smaller
	•	Structure becomes less defined, not more
	•	Never becomes a hard-edged circle
	•	Opacity increases slightly, but stays soft (fog-like)
	•	At the smallest point:
	•	Orb briefly nearly disappears (not fully gone)
	•	No sharp dot, no circle

Exhale behavior (orb expanding)
	•	Orb expands smoothly to the inner edge of the logo
	•	As it expands:
	•	Opacity decreases
	•	Texture becomes more diffuse
	•	Edges soften and feather
	•	Orb should touch the logo boundary but never pass outside it
	•	Orb fades gently near full expansion, not abruptly

Critical constraints
	•	Orb is always constrained inside the logo
	•	Orb never becomes a perfect circle at any size
	•	No speed changes mid-cycle
	•	No pulsing acceleration
	•	Breath > heartbeat (calm, slow, inevitable)

⸻

LOGO OPACITY RULE
	•	Logo opacity is fixed
	•	Logo does not change opacity with the pulse
	•	Logo does not react to orb brightness
	•	Logo remains constant visual anchor

⸻

“I HEAR YOU” TEXT INSTRUCTIONS

Initial state
	•	Text appears centered, above or slightly overlapping the orb
	•	Text color: soft off-white (not pure white)
	•	Opacity: 70–75%
	•	Font weight: light / regular (never bold)

Behavior
	•	Text remains visible during idle breathing
	•	Text does not pulse
	•	Text does not animate with the orb
	•	Text fades out only after first tap
	•	Fade duration: 600ms
	•	Ease: gentle ease-out
	•	Once faded, text does not return unless app is restarted

Readability rule
	•	Text must remain readable on both phone and desktop
	•	If contrast drops, increase text opacity slightly — never brighten background

⸻

WHAT NOT TO DO (IMPORTANT)
	•	Do not sync text to breath
	•	Do not speed up pulse on interaction
	•	Do not shrink orb into a dot
	•	Do not let orb disappear entirely
	•	Do not let logo brightness change
	•	Do not add secondary animations
